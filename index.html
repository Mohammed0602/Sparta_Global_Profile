    Final Year Project

    During Mohammed’s final year of his studies, his thesis was to identify whether unauthorized access on Unmanned Ariel Vehicles left a digital foot print. He picked up various skills during this period, for example the dangers of unauthorized access on wi-fi networks. He obtained a UAV which is commonly known as drone for the sole purpose of carrying out attacks. Once the attacks were executed, extraction methodologies were then implemented on the drone’s data using various forensic extraction tools. Once the extraction was completed an analysis of the drone’s data was then undertaken. Even though the data collected from the drone was inconclusive in relation to the thesis topic, he still aims to try this again with a more updated drone and better extraction tools.
